graW: A Web Interactive Environment to Support Undergraduate Courses

Abstract:
This paper presents a Web/Internet-based distance learning environment which allows users to form academic communities through Web. The main idea is to provide means to have virtual communities sharing the same space. In order to help the interactions among different users either from the same or different academic institutes, these communities are comprised of their own synchronous and asynchronous communication tools along with their pedagogical resources, in addition to others to support the invitation of guest users other than academic ones. Our goal is the academic communities; academic subjects of a given department of an institute, which are organized to provide the stated extension, taking advantages of the Internet and the tools developed using it. This paper focuses a particular view of the announced system, that is, an instance of environment oriented to support, on the web, undergraduate courses. The architecture and the functional aspects of this environment are presented.
